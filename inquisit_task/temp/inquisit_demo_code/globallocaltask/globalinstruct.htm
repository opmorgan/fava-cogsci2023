Task: Global Letter Shape

For the next task you are asked to respond to the global shape of the stimulus.

The global shape can be either an H or an S.

Example:

The overall or global shape of this stimulus is H.
In this case, press “H”.
The overall or global shape of this stimulus is S (whereas the individual building block or local shapes are Hs)
In this case, press “S”.

The stimuli will be presented only for a very brief duration.
Please, respond as fast as you can while making as few errors as possible.

Press [space] to continue.
